# Ransomware Incident Response Basics

Ransomware preparedness requires both prevention and incident response planning.

Preparation checklist:
- Maintain immutable, offline backups.
- Test restoration procedures regularly.
- Segment networks to reduce lateral movement.

During incident response:
- Isolate affected hosts quickly.
- Preserve forensic evidence and logs.
- Coordinate legal, security, and communications teams.

Recovery success depends on tested backups and clear escalation runbooks.
